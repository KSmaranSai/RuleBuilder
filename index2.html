RULE BUILDER

Assingment

Side effect rules are conditions where certain variables are calculated and set. Other decisions can have different variables being assigned.

Nested

Nested rules are typically used with Yes/No rules to decide when other rules should run or should not run.

Yes/No

These are rules that imply that the decision to be made should be a Yes or No. If any of these evaluate to No - then the decision is marked as “No”.
